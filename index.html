About Shakam

Shakam, which means 'vegetables' in Sanskrit, was created by Mrs. Kavita Kulkarni, a true Puneri vegetarian. Shakam is all about celebrating the tasty world of veggies and fruits.

About the Host

Mrs. Kavita Kulkarni, a passionate Punekar vegetarian, created Shakam to celebrate the flavorful world of veggies and fruits. At Shakam, we embrace a tropical-themed ambiance and offer a blend of traditional and modern vegetarian dishes to awaken your taste buds with delicious flavors.
